Implement a premium Quran Recitation Mode for Rah-e-Safia.

Objective:
Create a seamless, beautiful audio recitation experience integrated directly into the Quran reader without disrupting the existing reading interface.

## Audio Playback

Implement:

* Play
* Pause
* Resume
* Stop
* Previous Ayah
* Next Ayah
* Auto-play next verse
* Seek/Progress bar
* Playback speed (0.75x, 1x, 1.25x)
* Repeat:

  * Current Ayah
  * Current Surah
  * Off

---

## Reciters

Provide a Reciter selector.

Initially include high-quality reciters such as:

* Mishary Rashid Alafasy
* Abdul Basit Abdus Samad
* Maher Al-Muaiqly
* Saad Al-Ghamdi
* Abdul Rahman Al-Sudais

Structure the implementation so additional reciters can be added easily.

---

## Reading Synchronization

While audio is playing:

* Automatically highlight the currently recited Ayah.
* Smoothly scroll to keep the current Ayah visible.
* Remove the highlight when playback stops.
* Keep the highlight subtle and consistent with the Rah-e-Safia theme.

---

## Mini Player

Display a compact player at the bottom of the screen showing:

* Reciter name
* Current Surah
* Current Ayah
* Play/Pause
* Previous
* Next
* Progress indicator

The player should remain available while navigating within the Quran section.

---

## Full Player

Tapping the mini player should open a full-screen Recitation Mode with:

* Large Surah name
* Current Ayah
* Reciter information
* Playback controls
* Progress bar
* Repeat options
* Playback speed
* Reciter selection

---

## Behaviour

* Continue playback while reading.
* Resume playback after temporary interruptions where appropriate.
* Handle network failures gracefully with user-friendly messages.
* Do not interrupt playback unnecessarily when changing pages within the Quran.

---

## Performance

* Stream audio efficiently.
* Avoid downloading unnecessary files.
* Cache recently played audio where practical to reduce repeated loading.

---

## Design

* Match the existing premium Rah-e-Safia green theme.
* Use elegant animations.
* Keep controls clean and uncluttered.
* Maintain excellent mobile responsiveness.

---

## Technical Requirements

* Preserve all existing Quran functionality.
* Integrate cleanly with bookmarks, continue reading, translations, and Tafseer.
* Build reusable audio components for future expansion.

After implementation provide:

1. Files created or modified.
2. Audio source/API used.
3. Reciters supported.
4. Caching strategy.
5. Performance optimizations implemented.
